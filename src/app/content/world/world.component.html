Guild of Adventurers

The job of the Adventurer's Guild includes offering quests, handling requests from civilians to be posted as quests, and also taking charge of the ranks assigned to current adventurers. After an adventurer completes a quest, the guild offers the associated sum of money.

Arok

Small town situated on the edge of forest, which provides it with high quality wood, sought after in the area. Used to be barely worthy of note, until huge bronze dragon decided to settle in the hills north-west of town. This started a chain of events, that put Arok on the map of the world.
